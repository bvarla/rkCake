About - US

Raksha's Custom Cakes is fully licensed and insured caking place located in Aldie, VA. We are specialized in unique and delicious wedding cakes, chandelier cakes and special party cakes. Every cake is personalized and different according to one personality and dreams. We encourage and request you to bring your ideas so we can make that happen. We bake cakes and make our icing from scratch. Hence the end results looks and taste great at the same time. I am a baker, cake decorator, civil engineer, art lover and a mom. My interest towards construction and art made me take a step in to this cake decorating world. I took many cake decorating classes/courses and mastered caking skills. After creating unique cakes, I was unable to stop learning and creating more edible wonders. Cakes these days don’t sit in the corner of a party waiting to cut and eat. They are center piece of a celebration. If you want to customize and personalize your centerpiece you are at the right spot. Contact us to book your free consultation and tasting appointments
